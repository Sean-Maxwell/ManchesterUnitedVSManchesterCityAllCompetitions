Decision to not do Linear Regression¶

It was decided to not do linear regression as the former Manchester United Manager which this study is based on left Manchester United due to poor performances. These statistics wont accurately predict where Manchester United may finish in the Premier League because when the new manager arrives, it tends to change their play style i.e. Play football with more possession or counter-attack football.

Note: Please refer to ManUnitedComaparison.html to see the reasoning and conclusions. Due to a change of Manager this tweaked the purpose of doing the in game details.
